The archive and motile space

Achieving a multi-chain archive or collection is possible in the present but does have it's technical limitations when it comes to cohesion. Where tech falls short, art has not by connecting us all despite it's provenance. Broadening visibility by layering into exhibition spaces is an intentional future.

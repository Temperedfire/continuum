A narrative invloving sci-fi and the discourse of existence.

Units in Communion

2133 is a speculative fiction that captures the wonders of Many-Worlds Interpretation through dark and satirical themes. With a deep adoration for the interstellar sci-fi genre, Arc of Icarus narrates the occurrences of a post-human crew in the face of the unknown. The three units personify the fundamental elements of humankind, called to commune amidst the vastness of outer space.

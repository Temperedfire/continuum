Units in Commune

2133 is a speculative fiction that captures the wonders of Many-Worlds Interpretation through dark and satirical themes. With a deep adoration for the interstellar sci-fi genre, Arc of Icarus narrates the occurrences of a post-human crew in the face of the unknown. The three units personify the core aspects of humankind, who commune amidst the vastness of outer space.

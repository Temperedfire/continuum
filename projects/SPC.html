The ✱SPC archive is a program of showcase for emerging artists.

The archive and motile space

Achieving a multi-chain archive or collection is possible in the present but does have it's technical limitations when it comes to cohesion. Where tech falls short, art has not by connecting us all despite it's provenance. While this archive exists in digital hyperspace, layering into physical form is an intentional future.

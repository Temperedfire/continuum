The archive is a program of creation, documentation, and showcase for creative contemporaries.



The archive and motile space

Achieving a multi-chain archive is possible in the present, but does have its technical limitations when it comes to cohesion. Where tech falls short, art has not by connecting us all, despite its provenance. Giving more space to create, collect, document, and showcase.

The SPC Archive consists of independent studio releases, collaborative solo and group showcases, and events. Broadening the visibility of a global shift toward creative independence is an intentional now.

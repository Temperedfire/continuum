The ✱SPC Archive is a program of documentation and showcase for creative contemporaries.



The archive and motile space

Achieving a multi-chain archive is possible in the present, but does have its technical limitations when it comes to cohesion. Where tech falls short, art has not by connecting us all, despite its provenance. We propose the importance of creating space for artists, makers, and creatives alike. As part of this endeavor, 20% of revenue from the program will go to empowering independent artists and initiatives. Giving more space to create, collect, document, and showcase.

The ✱SPC Archive consists of independent studio releases, collaborative showcases, and events. Broadening visibility of a global shift toward creative independence is an intentional now.

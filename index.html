THE GRAND RIFT

As a studio, we are driven by the interconnected elements of creation. Our approach to Web3 explores the intersection of digital and physical mediums and creates space for works to exist in various ways of their own.


What is 2133?

2133: The Grand Rift is a visual narrative project that seeks to capture the wonders of Many-Worlds Interpretation through dark and satirical themes. Founder and visual artist, RAE, created this passion project to share her adoration of interstellar sci-fi. Making the shift in art direction was the first, huge step!
